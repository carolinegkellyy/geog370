Homework 7: Electric Vehicle/Hybrid Vehicle Registration across Europe

By Caroline Kelly

I present a EV/Hybrid car map since I'm an Environmental Studies major who's interested energy consumption across nation's. There's a very high EV/Hybrid rate per capita within Europe, one of the largest in the world. I wanted to compare nation's in Europe and draw potential conclusions into why some nations have far more EV/Hybrid registrations than others. Electrification of vehicles is a crucial step in combatting climate change since there are zero net tailpipe emmissions of harmful air pollutants, like nitrogen oxide. In many ways, Europe can act as a blue print for a future economic shift within the United States from gas consuming vehicles to more electrified ones. The data used for this project was collected from EuroStat.
